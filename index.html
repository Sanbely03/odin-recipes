Spicy Sausage Balls

These sausage balls are as good as Bisquick sausage balls, but without the carbs and gluten. They are also keto friendly! Eat them fresh out of the oven or refrigerate or freeze for later use.

Submitted by Sani Bello Buhari Updated on December 30, 2024

Ingredients needed for making the recipes

  • 1 pound bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 1 cup shredded sharp chedder cheese or more to test
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 tablespoon coconut flour
  • 2 (.25 ounce) packages unflavored gelatin
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Steps in making the recipes

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F(165 degrees c) line a jelly roll pan with parchmentpaper
  2. Combine sausage, Cheddar cheese, almond flour, coconut flour, gelatin, egg, and baking powder in a large bowl. Mix to combine using your hands, a stand mixer, or a food processor. Form mixture into uniformly-sized balls (about 1 Â½-inch diameter) and place on the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, 23 to 28 minutes. Remove sausage balls and let cool on the pan until oil is reabsorbed, about 5 minutes.
